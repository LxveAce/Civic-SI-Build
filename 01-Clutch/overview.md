## Why This Mod

At ~170k miles, the stock clutch was worn and needed replacement. The Exedy OEM Stage 1 is a direct OEM-spec replacement — same friction material, same clamping force, same feel as the factory clutch. It restores the clutch to like-new condition without changing the driving character.

## Notes

- Paired with the stock dual-mass flywheel
- This is a maintenance item as much as a modification — the stock clutch was simply worn out
- The OEM-spec disc is well suited for the current power level (stock K20Z3 + intake)
- If power mods increase significantly (turbo, supercharger), a higher-rated clutch would be needed
- Works well with the Acuity short shifter and Hybrid Racing engine mounts for an overall improved shift feel
