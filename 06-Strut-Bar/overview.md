## Why I Want This

A front strut tower brace connects the two front strut tower tops to stiffen up the chassis. Less body flex during cornering means better front-end response and sharper turn-in. On the 8th gen Civic with its MacPherson strut front suspension, a strut bar is one of the most effective and easiest chassis stiffening mods you can do.

## Why I'm Not Diagnosing the Clearance Issue Right Now

**Cross-reference to [14-Suspension/](../14-Suspension/).** I'm going BC Racing BR coilovers as part of my full suspension overhaul. The BR includes **pillow-ball camber plates** that replace the stock upper strut mount entirely — the strut top geometry on my car is going to change. Specifically:

- Stock upper strut mount = thick rubber isolator with a steel top cap, ~1" tall
- BC Racing pillow-ball camber plate = lower profile spherical bearing housing, different bolt pattern, different top-deck height

Any diagnosis I do on the Megan Racing bar TODAY against the STOCK strut tops is wasted effort, because the thing that fits (or doesn't fit) will be physically different once the BR is installed. Whatever is currently interfering may become a non-issue, or a different interference may appear where there wasn't one. There's no way to know until the coilovers are in.

**So: the strut bar sits in the box until coilovers go on.** Revisit fitment then.

## The Two Likely Outcomes After Coilovers

### Case 1: It Fits
Great. 20-minute install (2 bolts per side + center adjustment), done.

### Case 2: It Still Doesn't Fit
Once the coilovers are in and the strut tops are at their new geometry, I'll know exactly what's interfering because I can measure it against a known reference. Likely suspects at that point (after ruling out the stock mount as a variable):

- **Brake master cylinder / booster** — some aftermarket Civic strut bars clear on the sedan (FA5) but not the coupe (FG2). The BMC/booster sits in the path of some designs.
- **Bar was for the sedan** — Megan Racing occasionally cross-lists parts. I'll verify the MR-SB-HC06FU-1P part number is specifically the coupe unit.
- **I need a different bar entirely** — possibilities: Cusco Type OS, Ultra Racing front strut, or a custom bent one. The BC Racing camber plates have a specific bolt pattern that any replacement brace needs to match.

## What I'm NOT Going to Do

- ❌ Grind, bend, or cut the Megan bar to force fit against the stock mount. The coilovers will change the situation, and any modifications now will likely be wrong for the final geometry.
- ❌ Return it for refund. It's bundled with my clutch purchase and I want to give it a fair chance once the strut tops change.
- ❌ Buy a replacement strut bar today. No data yet on what actually fits my final suspension config.
