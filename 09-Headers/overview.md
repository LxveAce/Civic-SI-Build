# Headers — Under Consideration

## Status: Planned (Not Yet Purchased, Not Yet Decided)

## Why Headers

The stock cast iron exhaust manifold is the biggest single restriction in the K20Z3's exhaust path. Aftermarket headers are one of the best bolt-on modifications for this engine, providing 8-18 WHP depending on the header design.

Combined with the valved exhaust cutout (headers + open cutout = 15-25 WHP over stock), headers complete the intake-to-exhaust breathing path.

## Options Under Consideration

| Header | Design | Gain | Price | Notes |
|--------|--------|------|-------|-------|
| **Skunk2 Alpha** | 4-2-1 | 8-15 WHP | $650-800 | Best all-around. Great mid-range and top end. Excellent quality. |
| **PLM (Private Label Mfg)** | 4-2-1 Tri-Y | 8-15 WHP | $250-350 | Budget king. Solid performance, decent fitment. Stainless. |
| **DC Sports 4-2-1** | 4-2-1 | 5-10 WHP | $350-450 | Ceramic coated. Good quality, slightly less aggressive. |
| **Hytech (Bisi)** | 4-1 | 10-18 WHP | $800-1200 | Top-end monster. Sacrifices some low/mid torque for peak power. Best for high-RPM builds. |

## Decision Factors

- **Budget:** PLM at $250-350 offers 90% of the Skunk2's performance at less than half the price
- **Sound:** 4-2-1 headers have a raspier, more metallic note than stock. Combined with the open cutout, it will be VERY loud.
- **Tuning:** Headers require retuning both calibrations (daily and sport). The AFR shift between valve-open and valve-closed modes becomes more significant with headers.
- **Emissions (CA):** Very few K20Z3 headers have CARB EO numbers. In California, this could be an issue at inspection. In non-CARB states, no issue.

## Install Order

**Headers should be installed AFTER the valved exhaust cutout is working and tuned.**

1. Install cutout, get both tunes working
2. Install headers
3. Retune both calibrations on the dyno (strongly recommended at this point)
4. Verify AFR in both valve positions with the new headers

## Notes

- Headers change the exhaust note even with the valve closed (stock mufflers still in use). The car will be slightly louder than stock in daily mode.
- Header install on the K20Z3 is labor-intensive (tight engine bay, rusted hardware at 170k miles). Budget 4-8 hours or consider professional installation.
- PB Blaster the exhaust manifold studs several days before attempting removal.
