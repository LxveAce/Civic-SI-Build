# Maintenance Parts Catalog — OE + Aftermarket Part Numbers

| Fluid | Spec | OE Part | Aftermarket Equivalent | Capacity | Change Interval |
|-------|------|---------|------------------------|----------|-----------------|
| Motor oil | 5W-30 full synthetic (community), 0W-20 (Honda spec) | N/A (Honda doesn't brand oil anymore) | Mobil 1 5W-30 EP, Pennzoil Platinum 5W-30, HPL No-VII 5W-30 | 4.4 qt (w/ filter) | 5,000 mi / 6 months |
| Oil filter | K-series short | **15400-PLM-A02** | Mobil 1 M1-110A, Purolator PL14610 | 1 | Every oil change |
| Coolant | Honda Type 2 (blue, pre-mix) | **OL999-9011** | NONE — use OEM only | ~1.3 gal | 50-60k mi |
| Transmission fluid | Honda MTF-3 | **08798-9031** | NONE — stock Honda MTF only, synchros hate GL-4/5 | 2.2 qt | 30-40k mi |
| Brake fluid | DOT 4 LV | N/A | Motul RBF 600 (boiling point 594F dry / 421F wet) | ~1 qt (bleed) | 2 yr / 30k mi |
| Clutch fluid | DOT 4 LV | Same as brake | Motul RBF 600 (share bottle with brakes) | ~0.5 qt | 2 yr / 30k mi |
| Power steering | Honda PSF-S | **08206-9002** | NONE — Honda PSF-S only | ~0.8 qt | 60k mi |

**Aftermarket oil preference:** Mobil 1 EP 5W-30 or HPL No-VII 5W-30. I'm running 5W-30 over Honda's 0W-20 spec because of 170k-mile bearing clearances — the slightly heavier oil keeps pressure up.

**On E85:** oil change interval drops to **3,000-4,000 mi**. Ethanol combustion byproducts contaminate oil faster. Non-negotiable maintenance tax for running ethanol.

## Ordering Preferences

| Vendor | Best For | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Honda dealer (VIN quote)** | Genuine OEM where it matters (VTC actuator, gaskets, seals, head fasteners) | Order by VIN to get the revised part. |
| **majestichonda.com** | OEM online, discounted | Same dealer parts at 20-30% off MSRP. |
| **RockAuto** | Aftermarket (Moog, Gates, NGK) | Consistently the cheapest. Slower shipping. |
| **AutoZone / O'Reilly** | Fluids, same-day needs | Borrow-a-tool program is clutch for pullers. |
| **Amazon** | K&N, Hawk, Hybrid Racing, Acuity | Prime shipping worth it for weekend jobs. |
| **HeelToe Auto** | Skunk2, Hardrace, harder-to-find K parts | Honda specialist. Excellent customer service. |
| **K Series Parts** | Specialty K-series bits | Best catalog for Hondata/FlashPro accessories. |
| **Summit Racing** | ATI, AEM, QTP | Usually cheapest on performance parts. |
